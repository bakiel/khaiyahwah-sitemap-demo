    The Divine Vision (1966)

    [Image Placeholder: Representation of the Divine Vision or H.E. Ben Ammi]

    In February 1966, as H.E. Ben Ammi lay deeply troubled by the predicament of his people in America, the Angel Gabriel appeared with a divine message that would change history:

    "It is time to start the journey back to the promised land and to establish the long awaited kingdom of God that had been prophesied by Daniel the prophet in the second chapter and the 44th verse. And the year of the Exodus will be 1967."

    This 45-second vision contained the blueprint for liberation—a divine mandate to return to the Holy Land and establish Yah's Kingdom on earth. It came at a pivotal moment when social upheaval was sweeping across America, with riots erupting in cities nationwide.

    When a massive snowstorm blanketed Chicago in January 1967, H.E. Ben Ammi saw it as divine confirmation:

    "As I walked out of my home on that morning, it appeared as if the entire world stood still, for all of the great awesome power of the Gentiles had been brought to naught by an accumulation of little snowflakes."

    The vision aligned perfectly with prophecies throughout Scripture that foretold the return of the children of Israel to their homeland after a period of captivity. For the African Hebrew Israelites, this divine communication confirmed what many had felt spiritually—that their true identity and purpose lay beyond the confines of America.

    Within months, preparations began for what would become one of the most remarkable spiritual journeys of the modern era—the exodus from captivity and return to the Promised Land.
