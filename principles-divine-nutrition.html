The Transition and Breakthrough

The transition wasn't easy. In those early years, we faced nutritional challenges as we learned to balance our plant-based diet. Some of our babies developed health issues, and many adults experienced detoxification symptoms as centuries of dietary corruption were cleansed from our bodies.

The breakthrough came through divine guidance and the discovery of healing herbs like fenugreek, which Prince Gavriel introduced to the community. This bitter herb, which the Chinese philosopher said "blessed is the man who crosses its path," became a cornerstone of our nutritional approach, known affectionately as "Love Powder". Along with other natural foods, it restored our health and vitality.
