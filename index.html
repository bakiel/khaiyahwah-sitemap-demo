Our Prophetic Journey Home

For centuries, the children of Israel languished in spiritual death, scattered to the four corners of the earth as foretold by the prophets. Stripped of identity, disconnected from heritage, and bound in mental captivity, we endured the curses described in Deuteronomy 28—the bitter fruit of disobedience. Yet even in our darkest hour, Yah never abandoned His covenant.

In February 1966, the Angel Gabriel appeared to H.E. Ben Ammi with divine instructions: "It is time to start the journey back to the promised land and to establish the long awaited kingdom of God that had been prophesied by Daniel the prophet in the second chapter and the 44th verse. And the year of the Exodus will be 1967."

This 45-second vision ignited a flame that would lead hundreds from America to Liberia and finally to the Holy Land—fulfilling the prophetic timeline with remarkable precision. After nearly 400 years in captivity, the children of Israel have returned home, establishing Yah's Kingdom on earth not as metaphor but as tangible reality in the Village of Peace.
