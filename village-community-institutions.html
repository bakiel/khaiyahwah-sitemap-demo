Community Institutions

The Village of Peace is more than just residences; it is a fully functioning community supported by vital institutions designed to meet the spiritual, physical, educational, and cultural needs of the Saints according to divine principles.

The House of Life (Beit Khyeem)

[Image Placeholder: House of Life building or mothers/babies]

A natural birthing center where thousands of babies have been born according to natural methods, guided by trained Divine Healers. It stands as a testament to the effectiveness of Genesis Nutrition and divine health practices, achieving exceptional outcomes for mothers and children without reliance on conventional medical interventions.

Kingdom School in Holiness

[Image Placeholder: Kingdom School building or students learning]

An educational institution dedicated to preserving young minds from worldly corruption. It integrates spiritual values, Hebrew heritage, and Kingdom principles with academic learning, preparing the next generation to uphold and advance the community's divine mission.

Divine Dining Hall

[Image Placeholder: Community members eating together in the dining hall]

A central communal space where Saints gather for meals prepared strictly according to Genesis Nutrition principles. It fosters fellowship, ensures adherence to the divine diet, and reinforces the communal aspect of Kingdom living.

Botanical Gardens

[Image Placeholder: Gardens with herbs and produce]

Areas dedicated to cultivating healing herbs (like fenugreek) and organic produce used in Genesis Nutrition. These gardens symbolize the community's connection to the earth, self-sufficiency, and reliance on natural remedies provided by the Creator.

Cultural Arts Center

[Image Placeholder: Cultural performance, artwork, or the center itself]

A hub for preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of the Hebrew Israelites. It hosts musical performances, dance, visual arts, and other cultural expressions that celebrate the community's identity and spiritual journey.
