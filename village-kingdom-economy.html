Kingdom Economy: Building Self-Sufficiency

A fundamental principle of the Kingdom of Yah is self-sufficiency ("Do for Self"). The community strives to build a Kingdom Economy based on divine principles, sustainable practices, and collective well-being, rather than relying on external, often exploitative, economic systems.
