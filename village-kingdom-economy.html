Kingdom Economy: Building Self-Sufficiency

A fundamental principle of the Kingdom of Yah is self-sufficiency ("Do for Self"). The community strives to build a Kingdom Economy based on divine principles, sustainable practices, and collective well-being, rather than relying on external, often exploitative, economic systems.

[Image Placeholder: Taste of Life or Soul Vegetarian restaurant]

Pioneering establishments like "Taste of Life" in Israel and "Soul Vegetarian" abroad serve as both economic enterprises and ambassadors of Genesis Nutrition, offering healthy, plant-based cuisine to the public.

Artisan Workshops

[Image Placeholder: Community members crafting textiles, jewelry, etc.]

Workshops producing textiles, natural body products, crafts, and other goods provide employment and generate income while preserving traditional skills and promoting natural living.

Wellness Center

[Image Placeholder: Wellness center services or environment]

Offering services based on divine health principles, including nutritional counseling, herbal remedies, and other natural therapies, promoting holistic well-being within and beyond the community.

Sustainable Enterprises

[Image Placeholder: Organic farming, construction, or other enterprise]

Ventures in organic farming, construction, music production, and other fields aim to build a diverse and resilient economy aligned with Kingdom values.

The Kingdom Economy focuses on creating a healthy, self-sustaining community, providing meaningful work for its members, and offering products and services that reflect divine principles to the wider world.
